Hey, I'm Maia Kipping

I am a UK based content creator, founder of Creative Evolution Ltd and all round creative. I have a background in film and TV, from screenwriting to directing. My journey has been an exciting blend of modelling, social media management and content creation. My mission in everything I do is to help to empower brands in the digital space.

Modelling & Content Creation

When I'm not busy founding and leading Creative Evolution Ltd, you might find me in front of the camera, channelling my inner model. I have modelled for small beauty brands, worked with local photographers and have featured in Elle Italia when modelling for Stay Wild Swim. I also create unique and engaging content, including User-Generated Content (UGC), that resonates with audiences and brings brands to life.

Filmmaking & Writing

With a background in film and TV, storytelling is engrained in me. Whether it's crafting a compelling narrative or writing down my thoughts, I thrive on creativity and expression. Having gone to film school, I have a large understanding of storytelling through writing, videography, directing and editing. I gained a lot of experience in this world, from wardrobe assisting at the Peaky Blinders Festival to being a production assistant on Alan Carr's Epic Game Show. (My IMDB is here)

Founder of Creative Evolution Ltd

I'm the proud founder of Creative Evolution Ltd, a creative agency dedicated to supporting medium-sized businesses, especially those owned by females and people of colour. At Creative Evolution, we offer comprehensive marketing services, from digital marketing and social media management to videography and more. Our goal? To be your one-stop shop for all things creative!
