FILMMAKING

Film & Television Experience

I have a degree in Practical Filmmaking (BA Hons) from Met Film School. I have experience in writing, directing, production running and assisting. Alongside this, I had an internship at Fremantle Media, where I worked with the development team, worked as an edit assistant and was an office runner during that time. I also have had the pleasure of wardrobe assisting at the Peaky Blinders Festival in Birmingham.
